Key competitive advantages

Only combined platform
No competitor offers accelerator management (mentoring, LMS, community) + grant management in one product. Fluxx/SmartSimple are funding-only.
$25/seat FM entry point
FM admin seats at $25/mo make the Funding Module accessible to small organizations. Competitors bundle seats at much higher per-user costs. Platform seat revenue (€59/mo) stays completely untouched.
Capital calls = no competitor
Capital call management and investor tracking are Enterprise-only features that no GMS competitor offers. Key differentiator for VC/angel network segment.

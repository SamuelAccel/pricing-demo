Funding module — pricing strategy Updated May 12

Feature-unlock model: the Funding Module unlocks capabilities on top of existing admin seats. Seats are not bundled — they remain governed by the platform plan.

Pricing model — Feature Unlock (Option B): The Funding Module is a capability unlock, not a seat plan. It adds funding features to whatever admin seats the customer already has. Seats continue to be priced at €59/mo each through the platform plan. This preserves seat revenue and avoids giving away consumer surplus.
Terminology: "GMS" = complete suite (ATS + Funding Module). The add-on is the Funding Module. Compliance → Professional+. Audit logs → Professional+. Capital calls + investor tracking → Enterprise.
Seat economics: Current AA seat price = €59/mo. A customer with 10 seats pays €590/mo in seats alone. Under the old model (10 seats bundled in FM Pro at €599/mo), the entire Funding Module was essentially free. Under this model, €599/mo is pure feature revenue on top of seat revenue.

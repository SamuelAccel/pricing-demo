Funding module — pricing strategy Updated May 15

Feature-unlock model: the Funding Module unlocks capabilities in the FM environment. FM admin seats ($25/mo) are separate from platform seats (€59/mo).

Pricing model — Feature Unlock (Option B): The Funding Module is a capability unlock priced in USD. FM admin seats ($25/mo) grant access to the funding environment only. Platform seats (€59/mo) remain separate and cover all non-FM modules. A user who needs both pays both.
Terminology: "GMS" = complete suite (ATS + Funding Module). The add-on is the Funding Module. Compliance → Professional+. Audit logs → Professional+. Capital calls + investor tracking → Enterprise.
Why two seat types? Platform seats at €59/mo represent significant per-seat revenue. Bundling them into FM would give away consumer surplus. FM admin seats at $25/mo make the FM accessible while keeping platform seat economics intact. Customers who only need funding (no ATS) pay $25/seat. Customers who need both pay €59 + $25 = ~$84/user.

Key advantage of two-seat-type model: Platform seats (€59/mo) generate significant revenue. FM admin seats ($25/mo) are a separate, lower-cost entry point for the funding environment.
